Q2 2026 Market Notes - Internal Memo

The North region saw a sharp decline in Widget A sales during June, largely attributed
to a temporary supplier shortage that delayed restocking by three weeks. Field teams
report demand remained strong; this looks like a supply issue, not a demand issue.

The East region's Widget B performance has been steadily improving since March, helped
by a new regional distributor partnership signed in February. Leadership expects this
growth trend to continue into Q3 if the distributor maintains current fulfillment rates.

South region Widget A sales have been volatile month to month, swinging between strong
and weak performance with no clear seasonal pattern identified yet. The regional team
suspects local competitor promotions are driving the fluctuation, but this has not been
confirmed with hard data.

Overall cost ratios have crept upward slightly across most regions, largely due to
rising logistics costs. Finance recommends monitoring the cost-to-revenue ratio closely
heading into the second half of the year.
